The Problem

Knowledge shouldn't be this hard to find.

Every team creates mountains of valuable knowledge. Almost none of it gets used. It's buried, scattered, and forgotten.

⚡
Fragmented across 20+ tools

Slack, Notion, Jira, Confluence, email, Drive — critical context lives everywhere and nowhere.

⏳
30% of your day is lost searching

Hours vanish hunting for files, asking colleagues who already answered this last month.

🔄
Wikis decay the moment they're written

Every static knowledge base becomes a graveyard. No one updates it. No one trusts it.

😮‍💨
Decisions made with incomplete context

Without the full picture, even smart teams make slow, uncertain, costly decisions.

The Solution

One place to know everything.

Nexus learns your organisation continuously. Ask anything — get a real answer with sources, in seconds.

01 — Connect

Plug in everything

Link Slack, Notion, GitHub, Linear, Gmail, Drive and more in minutes. Nexus indexes all of it, silently, in real time.

02 — Ask

Ask in plain language

"What did we decide about pricing last quarter?" Get a direct, cited answer — not a list of links to skim through.

03 — Flow

Stay in your flow

Nexus lives where you work — inside Slack, your browser, or your IDE. Zero tab-switching, zero friction.

Our Mission

We believe the best moments at work happen when you're in flow — when you have exactly what you need, exactly when you need it. Nexus is built to make that the default, not the exception.

We're rebuilding institutional memory from scratch — real-time, trustworthy, and effortlessly maintained.
